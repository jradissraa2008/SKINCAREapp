Pink Tips 💅

💧
Layer Thinnest First
Toner → Serum → Cream → SPF. Thicker products block thinner ones from absorbing. Never reverse!
🌙
Never Sleep in Makeup
Foundation overnight clogs pores and speeds aging. Even if exhausted — cleanse. 3 minutes changes everything.
☀️
SPF Indoors Too
UV rays pass through windows. Apply SPF every single morning — this prevents 80% of visible aging.
🤲
Press Don't Rub
Rubbing tugs delicate skin. Always press and pat serums and moisturizers in. Skin stays calmer.
💤
Sleep is Skincare
Skin repairs 10pm–2am. 7–8 hours = glowing skin. Dr. Althea works magic during this window!
